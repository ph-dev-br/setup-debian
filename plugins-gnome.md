# Plugins GNOME
My plugins list than i use in GNOME. All installed via [Extension Manager](https://mattjakeman.com/apps/extension-manager).

- [AppIndicator and KStatusNotifierItem Support](https://github.com/ubuntu/gnome-shell-extension-appindicator)
- [Bluetooth Battery Meter](https://github.com/maniacx/Bluetooth-Battery-Meter)
- [Clipboard Indicator](https://github.com/Tudmotu/gnome-shell-extension-clipboard-indicator)
- [GSConnect](https://github.com/GSConnect/gnome-shell-extension-gsconnect/wiki)
- [Media Controls](https://github.com/sakithb/media-controls)
- [Quick Settings Audio Devices Hider](https://github.com/marcinjahn/gnome-quicksettings-audio-devices-hider-extension)
- [Quick Settings Audio Devices Renamer](https://github.com/marcinjahn/gnome-quicksettings-audio-devices-renamer-extension)
- [Search Light](https://github.com/icedman/search-light)
